Facts About Eagles

Scientific name of Eagle?

Haliaeetus leucocephalus.

Lifespan of Eagles?

Bald Eagle: 20 years, Crowned Eagle: 14 years.

Eagles habitats?

bald eagles use forested habitats for nesting and roosting

and expanses of shallow fresh or salt water for foraging.

Eagles favorite food?

Fish.

In 20 words describe an Eagle?

Eagles have amazing eyesight and can detect prey up to two miles away.they fly at up to 10,000 feet.
