Chameleon

Scientific name:Chamaeleonidae

Lifespan:Veiled chameleon: 5 years, Panther chameleon: 2 – 3 years, Jackson's chameleon: 5 – 10 years

Favorite food: crickets, roaches, silkworms, and superworms.

Habitat:They live in warm habitats that range from rain forest to desert conditions, with various species occurring in Africa, Madagascar, southern Europe, and across southern Asia as far as Sri Lanka.

Facts: chameleons do not modify their hues by accumulating or dispersing pigments within their skin cells, the researchers found. Instead, the lizards rely on structural changes that affect how light reflects off their skin.
